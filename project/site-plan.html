Teacher Training and Development Site Plan

Site Name

Professional Educators' Hub

Chosen to represent a resourceful platform for teacher effectiveness and professional growth.

Site Purpose

This website provides resources, strategies, and professional development opportunities to enhance teacher effectiveness and foster growth.

Scenarios

  • What resources are available for improving classroom dynamics?
  • Where can teachers find professional development workshops?

Color Schema

Dark Blue (#00334d): Used for headings and buttons to convey professionalism.

White (#FFFFFF): Used for background to ensure a clean, modern look.

Orange (#FFA500): Accents for links and highlights to add vibrancy.

Blue (#006699): For backgrounds of sections to create contrast.

Typography

Finlandica: For headings to ensure modern and bold visuals.

IBM Plex Sans: For body text to maintain readability.
